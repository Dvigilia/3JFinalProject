Admin Dashboard

A separte interface for administators to manage services, bookings, and therapists.

  • Manage Bookings:
    • Booking List: Table with all booking details, status and filters for pending, confirmed, and completed bookings.
    • Actions: Approve, cancel or reschedule bookings.
  • Manage Services:
    • Service List: List of all serviceds with options to edit or delete.
    • Add New Services: Form for adding a new service with fields for name, description, price, and duration.
  • Therapist Schedule Management:
    • Availability Calendar: Calendar view to see therapist availability and appointments.
    • Add Availability: Form to add or edit therapist working hours.
  • Payment and Reports:
    • Payments Table: List of all transactions with filters for paid, unpaid, and refunded.
    • Reports Section: Data visualization on bookings, earnings, and customer satisfaction trends.
