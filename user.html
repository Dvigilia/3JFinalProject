User Dashboard

Once logged in, users access a personalized dashboard for managing appointments, reviews, and account settings.

  • Upcoming Appointments:
    • List or card view showing upcoming appointments with details and a “Cancel” or “Reschedule” option.
  • Past Appointments:
    • List of previous appointments with an option to leave a review.
  • Account Settings:
    • Profile: Edit personal details like name, email, and phone number.
    • Change Password: Form for password update.
  • Promotions and Rewards:
    • Display any applicable promotions or loyalty rewards available to the user.
